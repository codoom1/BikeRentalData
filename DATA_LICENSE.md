# Third-party data terms

This package downloads source data to the user's own machine and does not
bundle the full processed dataset.

Capital Bikeshare trip data are provided under the
[Capital Bikeshare Data License Agreement](https://capitalbikeshare.com/data-license-agreement).
That agreement prohibits publishing, sublicensing, selling, or distributing
the data as a stand-alone dataset, subject to its complete terms.

Historical ASOS observations are retrieved through the
[Iowa Environmental Mesonet archive](https://mesonet.agron.iastate.edu/request/download.phtml).
Users should acknowledge the IEM and the underlying weather observations as
appropriate.

The package's MIT license applies only to original software, not third-party
data, trademarks, or the published article.
